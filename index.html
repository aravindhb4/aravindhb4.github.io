Trancity Chennai

  • Done at: Arivu Ventures
  • My Role: UI/UX Designer
  • Timeline: September 2016 - present
  • My work: Android App Design, Website Design and Branding/Promotions
  •    Playstore
  •    Website

Most of my work at Arivu Ventures went into this project. Trancity is a comprehensive app for commuters in Chennai. Trancity helps them find nearby bus stops/train station details and also the timings that they can plan ahead. The first version of the project went live in the 1st week of January 2017 and we are constantly working to improve the app based on user feedbacks.


Android App Design


We started with multiple sketches on paper and taking inspirations from online. There were other apps that offer commute info the users but the UI is not user-friendly and sometimes clumsy. We wanted to give a refreshing feel to the app as people will be looking at it many times a day.



We made a invision prototype to get a overview of the functionalities that we required. We drafted out the wireframe and started into developement. We had to iterate a lot in design to make sure the underlying data is presented in an intuitive way. And we are working hard everyday to improve the user experience.





Trancity android app combines reliable data with great user experience. Be sure to check it out if you are a Chennaite!


  •    Playstore

Website Design


We went with a material design template as everyone is familiar with and it will be easy for us to convey our app features easily. I'm responsible for the images/icons/gifs, color, typography, user experience and text content in the website. Took me 2 weeks to complete the website working with developers.



The website is fully responsive and reflects the brand color used in the mobile app.
